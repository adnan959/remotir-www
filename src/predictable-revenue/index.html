Got questions?

We get it! We want to be as transparent as possible but here's some more answers to questions we hear a lot.

What exactly does "done-for-you GTM" mean?

It means we handle your entire go-to-market—from strategy to execution. We write the copy, source leads, run outreach, take calls, and close deals.

How is Remotir different from hiring a sales agency or SDR team?

Most sales agencies stop at booking meetings. We go further—owning strategy, outreach, lead nurturing, and deal closing. Plus, we work across your entire funnel: from your messaging to your landing pages to your CRM setup. We're not just a vendor—we're your revenue partner.

Do I need to have a product launched to work with you?

Not necessarily. If you're pre-launch but have a clear market and offer, we can help you shape your GTM, build your funnel, and start testing early. Some of our best outcomes come from helping teams set things up right before launch.

What if I've already tried cold email or outbound before and it didn't work?

That's exactly why founders come to us. We don't just blast out generic messages—we audit your offer, messaging, and funnel to understand why past efforts didn't convert. Then we rebuild a system that actually resonates and closes.

How long before we start seeing results?

Most clients start seeing traction—replies, qualified leads, and early wins—within 3 to 4 weeks. That said, we treat this like a system, not a stunt. Our process is built to drive consistent revenue, not just one-off spikes.

How involved do I need to be?

We do the heavy lifting, but we still need your input—especially early on. You'll help us understand your product, ICP, and current funnel. After that, we'll take over execution, give you weekly updates, and loop you in where needed.

Is there a minimum commitment?

Yes—there's a 3-month minimum. That gives us enough time to onboard properly, build the system, and start generating real results. Revenue engines aren't built overnight—but we move fast.

How does your pricing and revenue share work?

Our model includes a monthly fee based on your stage (Startup, Growth, or Scale) and a 15% commission on closed revenue. We also offer add-ons like ad management, founder coaching, or landing page design depending on your needs.

Can you help redesign our landing page or messaging?

Absolutely. One of our core strengths is refining your offer and messaging so it actually converts. We also have Meta-level design support in-house to rebuild your landing pages when needed—especially if your funnel isn't converting.
